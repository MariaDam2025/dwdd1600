Who Were the Signers

Famous Signers

some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Independance include

Thomas Jefferson
The primary author of the Declaration and later the 3rd President of the U.S.
John Adams
A strong advocate for independence, later the 2nd President of the U.S.
Benjamin Franklin
A respected statesman, scientist, and diplomat.
John Hancock
President of the Second Continental Congress, famous for his large and bold signature.
Samuel Adams
A key revolutionary leader and cousin of John Adams.
Richard Henry Lee
Introduced the resolution calling for independence.

Each of these individuals played a significant role in the founding of the Unitedd States and is remembered for their courage in signing the Declaration, an act considered treasonoous by the British Crown.
